# Sorites Problem 52 of Example 9 in Book VII of Lewis Carroll's Symbolic Logic

# Sorites
not absolutely ugly -> kept in a drawing-room
encrusted with salt -> not dry
kept in a drawing-room -> dry
bathing-machine -> kept near the sea
made of mother-of-pearl -> not absolutely ugly
kept near the sea -> encrusted with salt

# Contrapositive
not kept in a drawing-room -> absolutely ugly
dry -> not encrusted with salt
not dry -> not kept in a drawing-room
not kept near the sea -> not bathing-machine
absolutely ugly -> not made of mother-of-pearl
not encrusted with salt -> not kept near the sea
